Common questions

FAQ

Is Firta really free?

Yes. Registering items, generating QR codes, and the public contact page are completely free. No credit card needed ever.

Does the finder need an app?

No. Any modern phone camera can scan a QR code and open the page in a browser. No download, no account, no friction at all.

Is my contact info safe?

Your contact info is only shown when someone scans your specific QR code. It's not indexed by search engines or shared anywhere else.

What if I lose my phone?

Use an email address as your contact info instead — or a secondary phone number. You can register with any contact detail you want.

How do I print the QR code?

After registering an item, click "Download QR" to save the image. Print it on plain paper, sticker paper, or use a label maker.

Can I register multiple items?

Yes — there's no limit. Each item gets its own unique QR code. Manage all of them from your dashboard at any time.
